Aeronautical Engineer: 233911

The ANZSCO code for Aeronautical Engineer is 233911



Job Description

Aeronautical Engineers who wish to migrate to Australia via Engineers Australia Migration Skills Assessment are in charge of designing and testing products for aircraft, spacecraft, satellites, and missiles, which includes activities such like prototyping and testing, formulating design requirements, reviewing proposals, and organizing teams from design to manufacturing.

Average Income

Aeronautical Engineers migrating to Australia through the CDR report for Engineers Australia Migration Skills Assessment earn an average annual salary of $ 101,490 for male employees and $ 121,339 for female employees.

Average Age

Males: 40.4 Females: 37.3 Persons: 40.0

Skill Level

The skill level of an Aeronautical Engineer for Engineers Australia Skills Assessment is 1

Specializations

Aeronautical Engineering Officer (Navy)
Aeronautical Engineer
Aeronautical Engineer Officer – Aeronautical (Air Force)
Aeronautical Engineer Officer – Armament (Air Force)
Aeronautical Engineer Officer – Electronics (Air Force)
Avionics Systems Engineer
Weapons Aeronautical Engineering Officer (Navy)

Assessment Authority

Engineers Australia

Caveats

No caveats apply to this occupation

The ANZSCO code for Chemical Engineer is 233111



Job Description

Chemical Engineers who wish to migrate to Australia through the use of Engineers Australia Migration Skills Assessment are in charge of supervising industrial processes and the fabrication of products which have undergone physical and chemical changes, as well as designing and providing specifications for chemical process systems and constructing and operating commercial-scale chemical plants. It is possible that you will be required to register or obtain a license.
